HOW TO PLAY

to get airable land that is plains you need to get water on it.
you can only get food from farming, berries, and hunting. you can only get berries from a berry farm (late game) or forests
forests can only be rebuilt if you have saplings which are hard to get

FARM DATA

people need 1 food per turn
every turn each farm tile makes 5 food
21 farms to feed the entire population

HUNTING DATA

each hunting trip can bring between 30 and 90 food
